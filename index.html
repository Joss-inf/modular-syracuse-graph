Exact Diameter and Primitivity Exponent of the Modular Syracuse Graph G_p

Transparency notice: This work was produced through iterative human-AI collaboration. The human author posed the questions and guided the exploration; formal proofs were generated computationally. The author does not have the mathematical background to independently verify every step. This page is a conjectural draft, not a peer-reviewed result.

Main claims: For the modular Syracuse graph on Z/2^pZ:

  • Diameter: diam(G_p) = 2p − 1
  • Primitivity exponent: exp(G_p) = 2p

The proof strategy uses the Bernstein–Lagarias conjugacy to map G_p to a subdivision of the binary De Bruijn graph B(2,p).
